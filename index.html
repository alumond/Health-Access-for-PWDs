M&E interpretation

Priority actions for the next management cycle

Critical bottleneck

Communication support is not institutionalized.

Sign language interpretation and accessible health materials are the clearest system gap. This weakens informed consent, diagnosis quality, and trust in public facilities.

Delivery risk

Physical access remains inconsistent.

Respondents repeatedly mention ramps, narrow doors, stairs, toilets, long queues, and lack of navigation support. Accessibility is not yet a facility standard.

Equity risk

Cost is suppressing healthcare use.

Financial difficulty is high even where insurance awareness exists. Subsidy design, enrolment support, and disability-specific benefits require immediate review.
